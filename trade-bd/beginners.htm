Now you can find any old items or can sell any of yours in this web site.
In a word, this is the web site where you can sell your old and unnecessary items and buy others' items that you need. You can sell almost anything here (illegal and explicit items are not included in 'anything' of course).

This web site works in a very simple manner, it actually makes the contact between the seller and the buyer. When the seller and the buyer get each other's full contacting information from this web site, they can simply make the trade by a head to head meeting. [To clarify more, just in case any kid is reading this material, the buyer and seller can meet in some place where they can exchange/trade]. The purpose of this web site is simply for making the contact between the seller and the buyer.

What exactly can I sell here?
You can sell almost anything. For example: Your PC, Laptop, any PC Accessories (Hard Disk, Mother Board, Printer etc), any Electronic Product (TV, Freezer, Washing Machine, Air Cooler, Sound System, DVD/VCD player, Camera, Camcorder, Mobile Set, Walkman, Discman etc), your vehicles (Bike, Motor Bike, Car, Bus etc). You can also sell your old Books and CDs (Audio, Video, MP3, Software, Game). In a word you can sell here almost anything you like.

How does the selling/buying take place here?
First of all the seller signs up with his full name and contacting address. Then he/she adds his/her items into our Item List. The buyer (who also has signed up with full name and contacting address) can view every seller's items in the list. When the buyer chooses and decides to buy an item the contacting information of the seller is sent to the buyer. On the same time the seller also receives a notification about his/her item. The seller can then also view the buyer's contacting information. This is the part that the sellers and buyers do in this site. After that they can contact with each other to meet somewhere and exchange/trade the items. This is how simple it is.

All signed up users can be both seller and buyer at a time. Every general user can add at most 12 items to the list and can reserve 2 items at a time. When the items are sold, the user can add/reserve more. Any user can cancel any sell or any reservation at any time. Please read the Rules & Regulations to know more.
