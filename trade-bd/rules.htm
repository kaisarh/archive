Rules & Regulations for members

  1. Every member must login at least once in a month. Otherwise his/her membership will be cancelled. His/her products will be removed from the Item List. But his/her login ID or email address will not be banned.

  2. User IP will be banned for 10 minutes if three consecutive login errors are found.

  3. A General member can add up to 12 items at a time. When these items are confirmed to be sold, he/she can add more.

  4. An item will be cancelled if not sold within six months.

  5. A Buyer can reserve up to 2 items at a time. When these items are confirmed by the seller then he/she can reserve more.

  6. Every trade must be confirmed by the seller within two weeks.

  7. A Seller can cancel any trade at any time.

  8. The buyer can cancel any trade at any time but if he/she cancels three trades in one year then his membership will be banned.

  9. If the trade is not confirmed within two weeks the trade will be cancelled by the system and will be removed from the Item List.

Note: Rules are subject to change with out any notice and the web master of this site has the privilege to make any kind of modification to this site or site content.
